# Dominique's Game — Concept (Explore phase, draft 2)

## The occasion
Dominique is Luke's twin sister, 28. She just graduated from medical school at
UNT Health Fort Worth (TCOM — green and blue colors). She's going to be an
anesthesiologist. She just got engaged to a surgeon (Ruthvik — spelling TBC)
and they moved in together in Lubbock, in the West Texas desert.
**The game is a congratulations on her graduation** — and a celebration of the
new life she's stepping into.

## The feel
- Playable in 30–60 minutes
- Top-down pixel game, Pokémon Red/Blue structure
- Stardew Valley aesthetic — warm, cozy, almost exactly that energy
- A bit of free exploring, but generally one direction to go
- Gentle challenges along the way; big celebratory sign/message at the end

## The world (Luke's first idea — three locations)
1. **Graduation in Fort Worth, TX** — opens with everyone throwing caps in the
   air outside, TCOM green and blue, classic Texas things everywhere. Luke's
   avatar introduces the game in-world: "Hey, I built this for you."
   Then she heads to the airport.
2. **Luke's Mansion (Chicago, surrounded by forest)** — Luke's dream house,
   made up; the whole family lives there. Luke gives her a tour.
   Dominique has her own closet (long-running inside joke).
3. **Lubbock, TX** — a small house in the desert where she lives with her
   soon-to-be husband. Final destination; the big congrats moment.

## The cast
- **Dominique** — the player. Loves knitting (yarn everywhere), reading on her
  Kindle (books everywhere), anesthesiology references throughout.
- **Luke** — fun, funny twin brother. Trolls her with inside jokes. Camera
  around his neck. "Like Harry Potter, but half Japanese."
- **Ruthvik (sp?)** — her fiancé, a surgeon. Lives in Lubbock.
- **Henry** — younger brother. Tall (make him REALLY cool and tall), long
  flowing hair, Japanese rising-sun fox tattoo, half Asian, has epilepsy.
- **Mom** — blonde, big artist, paints a lot. Always moving furniture and
  rearranging things. (Mechanic potential: the furniture literally moves.)
- **Dad** — always either lying on a couch or doing a very slow jog. That's
  the whole personality and it's perfect.
- **Boo-Boo** — Dominique's pet (bunny or cat? TBC) who died ~12 years ago.
  An homage somewhere in the world.
